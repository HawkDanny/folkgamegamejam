okay but really what is a folk game?
Basically any game you've played where someone taught you the rules from memory, using few materials. Like tag, hide and go seek, red rover, the floor is lava, and slap games.

why a game jam about folk games?
No one person really makes a folk game. They're games taught from person to person, each changing it to suit the current players better, and through this they reflect the cultures and communities that they are created in.
